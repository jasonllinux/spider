Programs Offered

Welcome! NYIT-Amman offers career-focused programs in variety of in-demand fields, including business, computer graphics, engineering, and information technology. All classes are taught in English so students must be able to speak and write in English.

Our Unique Location

NYIT-Amman has an undergraduate campus in suburban Madaba and a graduate campus in the heart of the capital. Madaba is an East Bank town known around the world for its historic Byzantine mosaics, and Amman is a blend of historic and modern culture and architecture, a popular location for international investors.

NYIT Celebrates Achievements of NYIT-Amman Class of 2012

Family, friends, and faculty cheered 180 graduates of the Class of 2012 at New York Institute of Technology’s graduation ceremony at its campus in Amman.

“You are now part of an international network of more than 92,000 alumni,” said NYIT President Edward Guiliano, who congratulated students and conferred degrees. “In addition to what you know, you will be rewarded for your ability to think, to leverage technology, and to apply your ideas.” Read More.

NYIT Team to Represent Jordan at CFA Institute Research Challenge in London

A team of four M.B.A. students team from the New York Institute of Technology (NYIT) Amman campus won the 2012 CFA Institute Research Challenge hosted by CFA Jordan.The winning team will now advance to the challenge’s Europe, Middle-East and Africa (EMEA) Regional Final on March 15, 2012, in London, where it will compete for a place at the Global Final on the April 11, 2012, in New York.
