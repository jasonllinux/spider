Give to NYCOM

NYCOM is in the midst of some enormously exciting transitions ... installing new technologies in our classrooms, adding new standardized patient and robotic facilities, upgrading our research resources ... all of which make for a dynamic time here at NYCOM. We want you to be an integral part of it. Please join us in transforming our proud past into an even more vibrant future.

Please contact the director of NYCOM's Development Office at 516.686.4007 to discuss naming opportunities and other ways you can support NYCOM in this exciting journey.

NYCOM’s Legacy Path
Buy your brick today!

We invite you to support NYCOM in a meaningful and lasting way through the Legacy Path. You can have your name or that of a loved one engraved on a brick to serve as a permanent symbol of your connection to NYCOM.

Your brick will be part of NYCOM’s Legacy Path and will be seen by thousands of students and visitors as they walk through the lovely grounds of the NYCOM Quad.

Buying a brick is an ideal way to commemorate your years at NYCOM, remember a loved one or pay tribute to a favorite professor. Consider a brick as a wonderful gift for a special occasion such as birthday, anniversary or graduation.
