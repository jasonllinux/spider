Service Central

Service Central would like to welcome you to our service request Web pages. We are responsible for providing technical support to faculty and staff on campus.

 

 

We seek to provide prompt, courteous, and accurate technical support while upholding the computer policies and procedures set forth by New York Institute of Technology. Service Central is a department of Information Technology and Infrastructure.

 

Faculty and Staff: Login problems with new myNYIT portal? Follow these instructions.

 

 

Service Central's Fall 2012 hours of operation starting 9/5/2012: 

  • Monday - Thursday 8 a.m. - 11:00 p.m.
  • Friday 8a.m. - 6 p.m.
  • Saturday 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
  • Sunday closed

 

 

 

Contact Us:

For expedited assistance with your Desktop Support, Academic Computing, Telecommunication, Media Services and Facilities needs contact Service Central at 516.686.1400 (ext. 1400 on campus) or servicecentral@nyit.edu.
