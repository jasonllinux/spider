                                    Welcome

                                        IEEE ICCT2010 will be held in Nanjing, China on November 11-14, 2010. IEEE ICCT is a biennial conference series, focusing on both theory and applications mainly covering the topic of communication technology. In addition to the technical sessions, there will be invited sessions, tutorials and keynote addresses.
                                        The 2010 conference will be Co-sponsored by IEEE Beijing Section,  IEEE Nanjing Section and  Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications and technically co-sponsored by Institution of Engineering and Technology(IET), Chinese Institute of Electronics (CIE),China Institute of Communications (CIC),  All papers accepted will be included in IEEE Xplore and indexed by EI Compendex.
